You will now be given the option to either perform the task on your own as in the previous four rounds or to copy the choice of somebody else who has previously played this game. When you choose to copy another player, the computer chooses for you the same asset as this subject has chosen in the round to be faced of the corresponding block. Recall that the last round of each block is paid with certainty if this block is chosen for payment.

The screen contains information about the decision you would need to take if you decide to proceed on your own. We will also provide you with a list of subjects who have previously player this game. To make the best possible decision we will also provide you with information on their investment choices in the previous five rounds.

Payment is the payment player received after participation in the experiment. Note that Payment does not determine the payment you will get, as it depends on the (unknown) choice of the subject and the random choice of the block and rounds for payment.

Risk rating is the level of risk subjects took in their choices. Risk ratings correspond to the High, Medium, and Low levels.

Quality rating indicates the “efficiency” of the player's choices. Quality of decisions classified as High and Low .
