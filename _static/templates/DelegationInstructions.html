You have now the option to ìcopyî one of the subjects on the list. When you copy a subject, the computer chooses for you the same asset and holding decisions as the chosen subject. To help you make the best possible decision we provide you with the information about the other subjects.

Payment is the payment subject recived after the Block 1. Note that Payment does not determine the payment you will get, as it depends on the coin flip and random selection of the decision round chosen for payment.

Risk rating is the level of risk subject chosen in the Block 1. Risk ratings take the values of A, B, C, and D, where A corresponds to the least risky choice and D to the most risky choice.

Quality rating is the level of the quality of decision in the Block 1 in terms of maximization of the expected payment. Quality ratings take the values of A, B, C, and D, where A corresponds to the highest quality and D to the lowest quality choice.
