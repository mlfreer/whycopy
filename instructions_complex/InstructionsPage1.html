The next screens explain how to play the investment game in Block 1. Remember, a considerable amount of money is at stake so please pay careful attention to the instructions.

In this game, you will make 5 decisions. In each of these decisions, you can choose one of the two investment options. The return of your investments depends on the toss of a coin (simulated by the computer).

At the end of the experiment, one of the decisions made in the first block will be randomly chosen and will determine your payment. This amount of money will be paid in addition to the initial endowment of Â£2.5. In total, the first block will take about 25 minutes.

The table on the decision screens will show the payoff if you have chosen to invest in this asset. Options specify the payoffs for heads and tales, which are equally likely and determined by the simulation of the coin flip. For instance, each unit of the Option 1 would pay 90 tokens if the coin comes up heads and 10 tokens if the coin comes up tales.
